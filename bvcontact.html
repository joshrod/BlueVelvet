About

Blue Velvet Lounge has the best martinis in Madison. Our retro styling and a young, upscale crowd impart a certain sophistication to the atmosphere. We offer a cool, comfortable lounge great for cocktails and conversation.

We are definitely a place to come to if you're in a large group or would like to have a private party. We have plenty of room available with our upstairs "Tangerine Lounge".
